🔎 Why Strong Passwords Matter

Passwords are often the only barrier between hackers and your private information. Using weak or repeated passwords makes you an easy target.

  • 💥 81% of data breaches are caused by weak or stolen passwords.
  • 🔐 Strong passwords reduce your chances of account compromise dramatically.
  • 🌐 Hackers use automated tools to guess millions of weak passwords per second.

⚠️ Common Password Mistakes

  • Using the same password across multiple websites.
  • Choosing simple passwords like "password123" or birthdays.
  • Saving passwords in plain text or screenshots.
  • Never updating passwords after years of use.

✅ Tips for Creating Strong Passwords

  • Use at least 12 characters or more.
  • Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special symbols.
  • Avoid using real words, names, or dates.
  • Consider using a password manager to store complex passwords securely.
